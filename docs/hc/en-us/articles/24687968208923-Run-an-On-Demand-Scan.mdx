## Purpose

This article outlines how to run an On Demand Scan to perform a one-time immediate data collection with a collector client. BHE administrators may use it during collector client deployment, one-time collections, or troubleshooting.

## Prerequisites

* Creation of one SharpHound Enterprise client, see [Create a SharpHound Enterprise collector client](/hc/en-us/articles/18346330027547-Create-a-BloodHound-Enterprise-collector-client)
* Logged in as a user role which is authorized to run an On Demand Scan, see [User Role Definitions](/hc/en-us/articles/16372343366939-Administering-users-and-roles#h_01H6P9MK1XME9F91NR95XJXR3Z)

## Process

1.  In the top right, click settings ⚙️ → **Administration**
<Frame>
  <img src="/hc/images/image-63.png" alt=""/>
</Frame>
2.  In the top left side, click **Manage Clients**
<Frame>
  <img src="/hc/images/image-64.png" alt=""/>
</Frame>
3.  On the client, click the burger menu and select **On Demand Scan** 
    * Tip: Verify the client is online by validating **Status** is **Ready**
<Frame>
  <img src="/hc/images/image-65.png" alt=""/>
</Frame>
4.  In the _On Demand Scan_ window, configure the scan:
    1.  **Data**: The type of data the schedule collects, see [SharpHound Enterprise Data Collection and Permissions](/hc/en-us/articles/9263138135963-SharpHound-Enterprise-Data-Collection-and-Permissions)
    2.  **Advanced Options**: See the [Scanning section in the article SharpHound Enterprise Tenant Configuration](/hc/en-us/articles/9295645381147-SharpHound-Enterprise-Tenant-Configuration#h_01GECVA74SF7JN7XRYPFBXASYW)
<Frame>
  <img src="/hc/images/image-66.png" alt=""/>
</Frame>
5.  Start the scan by clicking **Run**

## Outcome

The client will start the On Demand Scan after the next client check-in, usually within one minute.

Once started, the client's **Status** will show **Running a Job**:

<Frame>
  <img src="/hc/images/image-67.png" alt=""/>
</Frame>

After completion, see the job's status in the _Finished Jobs Log_ by clicking ⚙️ → **Administration**, and in the top left side, click **Finished Jobs** **Log**_. _The columns **Status** and **Status Message** report on the job's completion.

<Frame>
  <img src="/hc/images/image-68.png" alt=""/>
</Frame>
