The principal is granted the GetChangesAll right on the domain.

## Abuse Info

This edge is not abuseable by itself. When combined with [GetChanges](/hc/en-us/articles/17322126217371-GetChanges), BloodHound will create the abuseable edge [DCSync](/hc/en-us/articles/17322385609371-DCSync).

## Opsec Considerations

This edge has no opsec considerations.
